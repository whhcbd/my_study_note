## Part III 翻译 (Translation)

**Directions:** Translate the following Chinese sentence into English.

**中文句子：**
尽管人工智能在医疗诊断中展现出巨大潜力，但其伦理问题与数据隐私风险仍需严格监管。

**参考译文 (Reference Translation)：**
Although artificial intelligence shows great potential in medical diagnosis, its ethical issues and data privacy risks still require strict regulation.

**核心词汇解析 (Key Vocabulary Analysis):**
- **潜力 (potential):** n. the possibility of becoming something greater or better; 例句：The new technology has the potential to revolutionize the industry.
- **伦理的 (ethical):** adj. relating to moral principles; 例句：Ethical considerations are crucial in scientific research.
- **监管 (regulation):** n. an official rule or control; 例句：Government regulation ensures the safety of food products.

## Part IV 写作片段 (Writing Fragment)

**Directions:** Write a paragraph (about 120 words) on the given topic.

**写作主题 (Topic):**
Should universities replace traditional lectures with online courses? Discuss the advantages and disadvantages.

**范文 (Model Paragraph):**
Online courses offer flexibility and accessibility, allowing students to learn at their own pace and access resources globally. However, they lack face-to-face interaction, which may reduce student engagement and hinder the development of critical thinking skills. Traditional lectures, while less flexible, provide immediate feedback and foster collaborative learning through direct dialogue with instructors and peers. Therefore, a blended approach that combines online materials with interactive classroom sessions could maximize educational outcomes by leveraging the strengths of both formats.

**句型模板 (Sentence Patterns):**
- While [advantage], [disadvantage]. (While online courses are convenient, they may lead to social isolation.)
- On the other hand, [traditional method] provides [benefit]. (On the other hand, traditional lectures offer real-time interaction.)
- Hence, [balanced approach] is recommended. (Hence, a hybrid model is recommended for optimal learning.)
