### 题目原文（校园场景 - 选课咨询）

**音频文本**：

> **M**: Hi, Professor Chen. I'm a sophomore majoring in Computer Science. I want to take an elective course next semester, but I'm confused about the requirements. Could you give me some advice?
>
> **W**: Of course. Let me check your academic record first. I can see you've completed all your compulsory courses with good grades. For electives, you need to earn at least 8 credits, and you can choose from any department. However, I'd recommend you take "Artificial Intelligence Fundamentals" — it's not only relevant to your major but also counts toward your minor in Data Science.
>
> **M**: That sounds interesting. What's the difficulty level? I've heard it's quite challenging.
>
> **W**: Well, it does require some background knowledge, but nothing insurmountable. There will be weekly assignments, a group presentation, and a final project. If you manage your time well and attend the seminars regularly, you should do fine. By the way, the registration deadline is next Friday. Make sure you submit your application before then.
>
> **M**: Got it. One more question — can I still drop the course if I find it too difficult after the first two weeks?
>
> **W**: Yes, but there's a penalty. If you drop after the second week, it will be recorded as a "withdrawal" on your transcript, which might affect your scholarship application. So think carefully before you decide.

### 答案与解析

| 题号 | 答案 | 解析 |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | B | 男士明确说"I'm confused about the requirements"，并请教授给建议。 |

| 3 | C | 教授说"it's not only relevant to your major but also counts toward your minor"。 |

### 听力技巧提示

1. **场景词捕捉**：听到 elective, compulsory, credits, registration, deadline 等词，预判为校园/学业场景。
2. **数字信息速记**：记录关键数字（8 credits, next Friday, two weeks）。
3. **因果信号词**：注意 because, since, so, therefore 等因果关系词，它们常引出答案信息。
4. **but 后置原则**：听到"But"后的内容往往是考点，本对话中多个答案都在 but 之后。

## 二、阅读部分

### Passage: The Impact of Urban Green Spaces on Mental Health

**Word Count: ~420**

In recent decades, urban planners and public health researchers have increasingly focused on the relationship between green spaces and mental well-being. A growing body of evidence suggests that access to parks, gardens, and tree-lined streets can significantly reduce stress levels and improve overall psychological health.

A comprehensive study conducted across 18 major cities revealed that residents living within 300 meters of green areas reported 20% lower rates of anxiety and depression compared to those residing in neighborhoods with minimal vegetation. Researchers attribute this phenomenon to several factors. First, natural environments provide opportunities for physical exercise, which is known to stimulate the release of endorphins — the body's natural mood elevators. Second, exposure to greenery has been shown to lower cortisol levels, the hormone associated with stress.

However, the benefits of urban green spaces are not distributed equally. Low-income neighborhoods often lack adequate parks and tree cover, a phenomenon scholars term "environmental injustice." This disparity raises critical questions about social equity in urban development. City officials in some metropolitan areas have responded by implementing policies to increase green infrastructure in underserved communities. For instance, the city of Copenhagen has launched a initiative to plant 100,000 new trees in disadvantaged districts over the next decade.

Critics, nevertheless, argue that simply adding more parks does not address the root causes of mental health issues. They contend that factors such as affordable housing, employment opportunities, and access to healthcare are more influential determinants of psychological well-being. Proponents of green urbanism counter that environmental improvements should be viewed as complementary, not competing, with social interventions.

Despite ongoing debates, the trend toward integrating nature into urban design continues to gain momentum. Architects and landscape designers are experimenting with innovative concepts such as vertical gardens, rooftop parks, and "pocket forests" — compact ecosystems that can thrive in limited spaces. These solutions offer promising alternatives for densely populated cities where traditional parks may be impractical.

**4. 推理题**

**5. 态度题**

B. Neutral but acknowledges its validity

### 答案与解析

| 题号 | 答案 | 解析 |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | B | 文章主旨围绕绿色空间对心理健康的积极影响，同时指出分配不均的问题。选项A过于绝对，C、D与原文不符。 |
| 2 | C | 原文明确说"reported 20% lower rates of anxiety and depression"。 |
| 3 | C | 根据上下文"Researchers attribute this phenomenon to several factors"，attribute 意为"归因于"，即 assign a cause。 |
| 4 | B | "environmental injustice"定义出现在第三段：低收入社区缺乏绿色空间，即基于收入的不平等分配。 |
| 5 | B | 作者态度客观，既引用批评者观点，也呈现了支持者的反驳，显示中立但认可其合理性。 |

### 阅读技巧提示

1. **定位词策略**：遇到细节题先划关键词（数字、专有名词），回原文定位。本题"20%"是强定位词。
2. **词义题上下文法**：不认识的词可通过上下文逻辑关系推测，attribute 后接原因，选"归因于"。
3. **主旨题首尾法**：重点读首段和末段，本文的结论在末段首句。
4. **排除干扰项**：含绝对词（only, all, never）的选项通常不正确。

## 三、翻译部分

### 汉译英练习

**原句**：随着人工智能技术的快速发展，它正在深刻改变我们的生活方式和工作模式，但同时也引发了关于隐私保护和就业安全的广泛讨论。

**参考译文**：
With the rapid advancement of artificial intelligence technology, it is profoundly transforming our way of life and work patterns. However, it has also sparked widespread discussions about privacy protection and employment security.

### 要点解析

| 中文要点 | 英文表达 | 技巧说明 |
|----------|----------|----------|
| 随着...发展 | with the advancement of... / as...advances | 用介词短语或从句表达伴随 |
| 深刻改变 | profoundly transform | 用程度副词+动词的搭配 |
| 深刻地 | deeply / profoundly | 两者均可，后者更正式 |
| 工作模式 | work patterns | pattern 比 mode 更常见 |
| 引发讨论 | spark/arouse discussions | spark 更生动，arouse 更学术 |
| 隐私保护 | privacy protection | 合成词，privacy 作定语 |
| 就业安全 | employment security | security 比 safety 更正式 |

### 高分句型积累

1. **With引导的伴随状语**：With + 名词 + 介词短语/分词
   - 例：With the development of the internet, online education has flourished.

2. **However的转折衔接**：用于引出对比观点
   - 例：Online learning offers flexibility. However, it lacks face-to-face interaction.

## 四、写作部分

### 写作话题

**Directions**: For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write a short passage of about 80 words on the topic: **"The Impact of Social Media on Interpersonal Relationships"**.

You should state your viewpoint and use specific reasons and examples to support your idea.

### 写作技巧框架

```
开头段：现象描述
- 背景句：With the popularity of social media...
- 观点句：In my opinion, social media has both positive and negative effects on interpersonal relationships.

主体段：正反论证
- 正面：便利沟通、维系远距离关系
- 反面：减少面对面交流、表面化社交

结尾段：总结建议
- 总结观点
- 提出建议：合理使用社交媒体
```

### 范文示例（~85词）

**The Impact of Social Media on Interpersonal Relationships**

With the rapid development of social media platforms, the way people communicate has changed dramatically. In my opinion, social media has exerted a profound influence on interpersonal relationships, bringing both benefits and challenges.

On the one hand, social media enables us to stay connected with friends and family regardless of geographical barriers. We can share our daily lives instantly and offer support during difficult times. On the other hand, excessive reliance on online communication may weaken the quality of our face-to-face interactions, leading to a sense of isolation despite being digitally "connected."

Therefore, I believe it is essential to strike a balance between online and offline communication to maintain healthy relationships.

### 句型分析

| 高级句型 | 例句中的体现 |
|----------|--------------|
| With引导的伴随状语 | With the rapid development of social media platforms... |
| On the one hand... On the other hand | 对比正反两面 |
| 不定式作主语 | To strike a balance... |
| 现在分词作状语 | leading to a sense of isolation... |
| 宾语从句 | I believe it is essential... |
