> M: Hi, I'm calling about the course registration. I noticed that the advanced composition course I wanted to take is already full. Is there any chance there will be another section added?

### 听力技巧提示

## 二、阅读部分

### 题目原文（约400词）

**题目：The Hidden Costs of Digital Minimalism**

In recent years, digital minimalism has emerged as a popular philosophy advocating for reduced screen time and a more intentional relationship with technology. While proponents argue that this movement offers numerous benefits, a closer examination reveals some unexpected consequences that challenge its widely accepted premise.

The core principle of digital minimalism, as articulated by computer scientist Cal Newport, involves focusing on activities that support our values while deliberately eliminating digital distractions. This approach typically encourages individuals to delete social media applications, set strict time limits on device usage, and engage in analog activities such as reading physical books or taking walks without phones.

Research conducted by the Technology and Psychology Lab at Stanford University suggests that digital minimalism may inadvertently exacerbate certain social inequalities. Their study found that while professionals in knowledge-based industries reported improved productivity and mental well-being after adopting minimalist practices, workers in service-oriented sectors experienced heightened anxiety due to reduced accessibility to real-time scheduling applications and communication tools.

Furthermore, the movement's emphasis on analog experiences has sparked a concerning trend in environmental degradation. The demand for traditional paper books, vinyl records, and physical notebooks has led to increased deforestation rates and manufacturing emissions. Statistics from the Global Environmental Organization indicate a 12% rise in paper production-related carbon emissions since the digital minimalism trend gained mainstream popularity.

Perhaps most paradoxically, the rigid rules imposed by digital minimalism can paradoxically increase cognitive load for some individuals. Psychologists have observed that the constant vigilance required to resist checking devices creates what researchers term "decision fatigue" - a state of mental exhaustion that impairs subsequent decision-making quality. This phenomenon suggests that the effort of abstaining from technology may sometimes outweigh the benefits of reduced stimulation.

Nevertheless, proponents counter that these criticisms miss the point. Digital minimalism, they argue, is not about achieving perfection but about cultivating awareness. The goal is to develop a more conscious approach to technology that aligns with individual needs rather than adhering to rigid prescriptions.

As the debate continues, experts recommend a balanced approach that considers both the potential benefits and limitations of digital minimalism. Rather than adopting extreme positions, individuals should evaluate their unique circumstances and make informed decisions about their technology usage.

*(Words: 412)*

### 阅读技巧提示

1. **题文同序**：五道题按文章顺序排列，第1题通常在前两段找答案
2. **关键词定位**：划出人名(Stanford, Cal Newport)、数字(12%)、专有名词(exacerbate, decision fatigue)
3. **干扰项排除**：
   - 偷换概念：选项替换了关键词
   - 无中生有：原文明确没提到的内容
   - 过度推理：从原文中推得太远
4. **长难句分析**：找出主干结构(主谓宾)，注意 which/that 引导的从句

## 三、翻译部分

### 题目

**汉译英**（约30词，中等偏上难度）

**原文**：
随着人工智能技术的快速发展，它不仅提高了工作效率，也深刻改变了人们的日常生活方式，从交通出行到医疗健康领域都产生了深远影响。

**参考译文**：
With the rapid development of artificial intelligence technology, it has not only improved work efficiency but also profoundly transformed people's daily lifestyles, exerting a far-reaching impact on various fields ranging from transportation to healthcare.

**要点解析**：

| 语法点 | 原句表达 | 参考译文处理 |
|--------|----------|--------------|
| 主从复合句 | 多句并列 | 使用 with 复合结构作状语 |
| 递进关系 | "不仅...也..." | not only...but also... |
| 伴随状语 | 无 | 现在分词短语 exerting 作结果状语 |
| 范围表达 | "从...到..." | ranging from...to... |
| 抽象概念 | "深远影响" | far-reaching impact |

### 高分句型积累

1. **With 结构**：With the continuous advancement of technology, great changes have taken place in our daily life.
2. **Not only...but also...**：The Internet has not only facilitated communication but also reshaped commerce.
3. **Exert a profound influence on**：Modern technology exerts a profound influence on education.

## 四、写作部分

### 题目

**Directions**: For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write a short passage of about 80 words on the following topic.

**Topic**: The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Employment

**写作要求**：
- 讨论人工智能对就业市场的影响
- 至少包含两种观点（正面/负面影响）
- 使用高级连接词和复合句型
- 注意逻辑衔接和段落结构

### 范文（85词）

The rapid advancement of artificial intelligence has sparked widespread debate about its impact on employment. **On the one hand**, AI technologies have automated repetitive tasks, thereby enhancing productivity and creating new job opportunities in fields such as data analysis and machine learning. **On the other hand**, many traditional occupations face the risk of displacement, particularly in manufacturing and customer service sectors.

**From my perspective**, rather than viewing AI as a threat, we should embrace it as a tool for human progress. The key lies in acquiring new skills and adapting to evolving market demands. **In conclusion**, continuous learning is essential for everyone to thrive in the AI era.

### 句型分析

| 句型 | 在文中的作用 | 示例 |
|------|--------------|------|
| On the one hand... On the other hand... | 对比论证 | 展示利弊两面 |
| The key lies in... | 引出解决方案 | 强调重点 |
| rather than... | 表达取舍 | 观点转折 |
| In conclusion | 总结过渡 | 收束全文 |

### 高级连接词积累

- **Furthermore**: 递进
- **Nevertheless**: 转折
- **Consequently**: 结果
- **Nevertheless**: 然而
- **Thus**: 因此
