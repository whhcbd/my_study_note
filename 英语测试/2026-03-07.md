An airplane powered totally by the sun landed in Seville, Spain early Thursday after completing a 71-hour flight across the Atlantic Ocean.

The plane, a Solar Impulse 2 model, has just one seat. It left from John F. Kennedy International Airport in New York Monday morning.

This was the plane’s 15th stop in its planned around-the-world trip. The trip began in Abu Dhabi in March of 2015.

Swiss aviator Bertrand Piccard operated the solar-powered plane on this leg of the tour. The Solar Pulse 2 had an average speed of 70 kilometers an hour during his flight.

The plane’s power comes from 17,000 solar cells built into the wings. The cells capture the sun’s energy, which is stored in batteries on the plane.

The Solar Pulse 2 has a 72-meter wingspan. That is longer than the wingspan of a Boeing 747 passenger plane. The solar-powered plane weighs about as much as an automobile.

Piccard’s friend and business partner Andre Borschberg will operate the plane on the final part of the trip. He plans to fly from Seville to Abu Dhabi in the United Arab Emirates.

I’m Anne Ball.

## 翻译部分

### 中文句子
随着大数据和人工智能的深度融合，各行各业正在经历深刻的数字化转型。

### 参考译文
With the deep integration of big data and artificial intelligence, all industries are undergoing profound digital transformation.

### 重点词汇/句型解析
- **深度融合**：deep integration（也可用seamless convergence）
- **大数据**：big data（固定术语，不可直译为large data）
- **人工智能**：artificial intelligence（缩写AI需在全文首次出现时给出全称）
- **各行各业**：all industries（也可用various sectors）
- **经历**：are undergoing（现在进行时强调持续过程）
- **深刻的**：profound（比significant/deep更显书面化）
- **数字化转型**：digital transformation（当前热点术语）

## 写作片段

### 写作话题
**观点对比型**：有人认为人工智能将取代大量人类工作，导致失业危机；另一些人则认为人工智能会创造新的就业机会，提升生产效率。请阐述你的看法。

### 范文（约110词）
I believe that artificial intelligence will ultimately create more jobs than it displaces. While AI may automate routine tasks in manufacturing and data processing, it simultaneously generates demand for new roles such as AI trainers, ethics auditors, and human‑machine collaboration specialists. Historical precedents—like the Industrial Revolution—show that technological advances initially disrupt labor markets but eventually lead to higher productivity and novel occupations. Therefore, the key challenge is not resisting AI but investing in education and retraining programs to equip workers with the skills needed for the AI‑driven economy. A proactive approach can turn potential disruption into sustainable growth.

### 写作要点分析
1. **立场明确**：首句直接亮明观点，避免模棱两可。
2. **辩证分析**：承认AI会替代某些工作，但强调其创造新岗位的能力。
3. **历史类比**：引用工业革命的例子，增强论证说服力。
4. **对策建议**：提出教育、再培训等具体措施，体现思考深度。
5. **词汇亮点**：displaces, generate demand, historical precedents, disrupt, proactive approach, sustainable growth 等学术表达。
