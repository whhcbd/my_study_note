## Part III 翻译 (Translation)

**Directions:** Translate the following Chinese sentence into English.

**中文句子：**
鉴于人工智能在医疗诊断领域的突破性进展，各国政府亟需制定相应的伦理准则，以确保其应用既安全可靠又符合人道主义原则。

**参考译文 (Reference Translation):**
Given the groundbreaking progress of artificial intelligence in medical diagnosis, governments worldwide urgently need to formulate corresponding ethical guidelines to ensure its application is both safe and reliable while conforming to humanitarian principles.

**核心词汇解析 (Key Vocabulary Analysis):**
- **突破性进展 (groundbreaking progress):** n. significant advancement that represents a major step forward.
- **医疗诊断 (medical diagnosis):** n. the process of determining which disease or condition explains a person's symptoms.
- **伦理准则 (ethical guidelines):** n. principles or standards that govern appropriate conduct in a professional context.
- **人道主义原则 (humanitarian principles):** n. fundamental beliefs centered on human welfare and dignity.

**句型结构 (Sentence Structure):**
- **状语从句:** “Given...”引导的短语表示原因或条件。
- **复合谓语:** “need to formulate”表达必要性动作。
- **目的状语:** “to ensure...”表示制定准则的目的。
- **并列结构:** “both...and...”连接两个形容词，强调双重属性。
